Context

This will explain what Fate is

The Fate series is a collection of games, anime, and books based on the original visual novel Fate/Stay night. The stay night visual novel has three sperate routes which are played in this order: Fate, Unlimited Blade Works, and Heaven's Feel. Each route has it's own anime adaptation.

Spinoff Context

Everything aside from what I showed above, and a few OVAs, is a spinoff and can be ignored. The spinoffs are all completely unique stories that can be enjoyed by themselves. The most recommended spinoff is Fate/Zero as it is closest to the main line series. Zero should be watched AFTER main line series, but you can watch it before if you want.

I will put any spinoff that I can find at the bottom of this list.

Spinoffs

The spinoffs should be watched after you complete the main line series. The spinoffs can be watched however you want, but some do have orders that they follow. I will list the spinoffs and put the images in the correct watch order

Fate/Zero

The most recommended and debatable

Should Watch

Considered the main series prologue. Many people start the Fate series with this entry not knowing that it is a spinoff. Builds on some characters from the main series.

Today's Menu for the Emiya Family

Should Watch

A cooking spinoff featuring the cast from the main series. It is a silly slice of life with short 10 minute episodes. Despite it's nature, it still had a few parts that made me sad.

Fate/Extra

Recommended

Adapts the Fate/Extra game. Adapted by Shaft, meaning it is creative and ambitious, but the plot can be harder to follow.

Fate/strange fake

Should Watch

Grail War, but in America. Gil is my guy.

Fate/Apocrypha

Recommended

Grail war but x2. Introduces some of my favorite characters to the series (Ruler and Astolfo), but also some of my least favorite (Assasian of Black and Berserker of Black). Features a fight with MS Paint man.

Fate/kaleid liner Prisma☆Illya

Not Recommended

I cannot in good faith recommended this spinoff to "normal" people. It is magical girl fate. It does have some decent character development and an ok plot, but also a lot of questionable loli content. Wath the Vow in Snow movie and skip the rest.

Lord El-Melloi II's Case Files

Should Try

Waver spinoff. I love Waver, but am not a fan of his spinoff.

Fate/Prototype

Should Try

Original Fate concept. Shirou is a girl, Saber is a guy.

Fate/Grand Order

Watch If You Play The Game

Gacha game lore. Guadako is funny.

Carnival Phantasm

Main series and a few spinoffs have silly crossover episodes with other series from the Nasuverse. These should be watched after you complete the main series and each respective spinoff.

Content I Missed

I skipped OVAs for both the main series and spinoffs. Most of the OVAs are nonessential filler / supplemental content.
