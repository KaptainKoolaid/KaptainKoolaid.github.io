Visual Novels

Visual novels that I have played and briefly what I think about them

Steins;Gate

9/10 | HRs:60+

First true VN that I "beat". Still need to get the true ending and would like to get 100% achievements. Amazing VN, but need to replay now that I have played more VNs.

Fruit of Grisaia

9/10 | HRs:65+

VN that got me into the genre and possibly my favorite VN. Still need to finish Makina's route for completion.

Loved all the routes and main characters, except for Yumiko. All the other characters had disturbing bad endings and good endings that made me emotional. Sachi's route is my favorite, but Michiru puts up a fair fight for my favorite heroine.

Muv Luv Trilogy

8.5/10 | HRs:85+

All but two routes in Extra/Unlimited and four testaments left in Alternative before 100%.

Phenomenal game with an amazing story, profound themes, and immersive engine. I have yet to play another VN where they have sprites of characters backs and sides so that they can turn around or have side views. It was hard for me to play other VN games for a while because they were not as immersive. Muv Luv > AoT Isayama may need to site his source.

Song of Saya

9/10 | HRs:7+

The most disturbed and uncomfortable I have ever felt playing a VN. Feels like a H.P Lovecraft story.
